ABOUT SAE MNIT

The Society of Automotive Engineers (SAE) at MNIT Jaipur is a collegiate chapter dedicated to the advancement of mobility engineering. We are building a community of passionate engineers, designers, and innovators.

Our mission is to bridge the gap between theoretical knowledge and practical application. We are preparing to participate in national and international design competitions.
