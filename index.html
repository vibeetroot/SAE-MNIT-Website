About SAE MNIT

What we do

SAE MNIT is a student society focused on automotive systems, vehicle design, CAD, manufacturing, electronics integration, testing, and motorsport. We mentor projects, build competition cars, and host workshops.

Engineering Focus

  • Structure & Chassis Design
  • Drivetrain & Powertrain
  • Aerodynamics & Suspension
  • Embedded Systems & Telemetry

Vision & Mission

Mission

To educate, design and produce competitive student-built vehicles while building engineering skills through hands-on experience.

Vision

To be a leading university team in vehicle engineering, innovation and interdisciplinary collaboration.
